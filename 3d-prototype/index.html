VOXERIA 3D

A first walkable prototype of Voxeria in 3D — same blocks, names and biome colors as the original, just a brand-new perspective. Mine blocks to fill your inventory, then place them back down. Not all features exist yet, this is a taste of what's possible.

W A S D — move

Space — jump
Shift — sprint
Left click — mine block
Right click — place block
1–9 — select inventory slot
