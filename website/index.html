The game

What awaits you in Voxeria

A handcrafted pixel world full of secrets — solo or with friends.

🌍

Diverse biomes

Forest, desert, snow, jungle, volcano, ocean and the mystical Void — every world looks different.

⛏️

Build & mine

Mine blocks, gather resources and build your own structures.

🌋

Dimensions

Travel between the Overworld, a lava dimension and more — each with its own dangers and loot.

🏯

Dungeons & shrines

Hidden ruins, towers, enchanter shrines and dungeons full of rare ores are waiting to be discovered.

👥

Multiplayer & chat

Play with others in the same world and chat (13+, with filter).

📜

Quests & achievements

Complete challenges and unlock achievements while exploring the world.

🛒

Shop & upgrades

Upgrade your gear and unlock useful abilities.

🏆

Daily treasure hunt

A new hidden treasure every day — whoever finds it fastest climbs the ranking.
